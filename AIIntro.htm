I am working on a project for Artificial Intelligence for news articles. About 10 years ago I wrote some software for myself, just for my own use, to read some news web sites. It showed me only the articles that were new so I didn't have to look through a list of headlines that I've already seen. I've started a new project, written in C++, to use news data for AI.

I want to get both Democrat and Republican news. I want it to learn how to identify whether it is Republican or Democrat news. I am using Fox News as my source of Republican news and MSNBC as my source of Democrat news.
